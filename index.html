01

A Collective

of Creators

Were a collective of seasoned Burners and adventurous souls from the queer Burning Man diaspora.

At Love Burn, we blend the best elements from our respective Burning Man camps to form a unique music-focused queer community. Whether youre a long-time Burner or new to the experience, our camp is a place for connection, creativity, and freedom.

Our goal is to create a high-energy, welcoming space that celebrates the joy and open-heartedness found in PLUR rave culture joined with our free-spirited queer experience.

02

Our

Experience

Our camp is a modern take on classic rave culture, embodying euphoric energy, inclusivity, and a little bit of edge .

We celebrate queer culture, PLUR, and a shared love for music and connection. Think kind, open-hearted, and welcoming with a dash of the unexpected.

Its a space where everyone can be fully themselves, whether dancing euphorically or having quieter, deeper moments.
03

Our

Sound

We program with intention, weaving the familiar and the future. From hypnotic progressive house to mod disco, our music is a journey into both nostalgia and newness.

Nightime is our peak. Expect rave-inspired sonic journeys equal parts heady and euphoric.
During the day, we dial back to a hazy, Miami-meets-Playa chill, offering a backdrop for relaxed socializing and community building.
04

Our

Values

Our camp is rooted in kindness, openness, and the 10 Principles.

We believe in being fully present, leaving behind social media distractions to connect in real timeespecially on the dance floor. Guided by PLUR, we create a space for genuine connections, free expression, and celebrating diversity.

Whether you're a camper or a stranger-turned-friend, you belong here.
